WHO_WE_ARE

Founded in the spirit of the Weimar era's radical intellectualism, Weimar Labs is a non-profit collective of 24 core researchers. We operate at the intersection of privacy rights, network security, and open-source advocacy.

We aren't just a firm; we are a community. Our members come from backgrounds in cryptography, linguistics, and systems engineering, united by a singular focus on protecting the digital commons.
